Our Activities in Action

At Norton University, past Student Union events have left a lasting impact on both the campus and local community.


Team event

Our Team Event last year saw an impressive turnout, with students from different departments collaborating in fun challenges that strengthened friendships and created a strong support network.



Campus clean-up

The Campus Clean-Up initiative made a real difference, as volunteers collected over 500 pounds of waste, leading to a greener, more welcoming campus environment and inspiring an ongoing commitment to sustainability.



Charity event

The Charity Event was a resounding success, raising over $10,000 for local shelters and food banks, showcasing the generosity and compassion of our student body.



Cultural festival

Finally, the Cultural Festival was a vibrant celebration of diversity, drawing hundreds of attendees who enjoyed music, food, and performances from around the world, leaving participants with lasting memories and fostering greater cultural awareness and respect.
