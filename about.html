About Norton University Students Union

The Norton University Students Union (SU) serves as the voice of the student body, representing their interests and organizing a range of services and activities. We aim to create an inclusive community where every student can thrive both academically and socially.

Our Mission

To empower students by advocating for their rights, offering services, and creating opportunities for engagement, leadership, and personal development.

Our Vision

To foster a new supportive and inclusive environment where every student has the resources they need to succeed, both during their time at Norton University and beyond.
